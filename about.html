About

Context behind every connection

Subtex was built to capture the story behind the people you meet — and to turn that everyday networking into meaningful insight for individuals, teams, and organizations.

Why we built Subtex

A connection is more than a name on a card.

Every introduction carries a story — where you met, what you talked about, why it mattered. Yet that context is exactly what slips away first. A week later the name remains, but the meaning behind the connection is gone.

We created Subtex to fix that. Instead of letting your network flatten into a list of contacts, Subtex preserves the context behind each connection — the conversations, the moments, and the details that make a relationship worth remembering. It keeps the "why" attached to the "who," so your relationships stay as rich as the day you made them.

From there, Subtex goes a step further: it transforms those everyday interactions into advanced networking insights. By understanding the patterns across your connections, Subtex helps you see your relationships more clearly, follow up at the right moments, and turn a scattered web of contacts into a genuine, navigable network.

Built for the way people really connect

Insight that scales — from one person to an entire organization.

The value of context compounds as more people share it. That's why Subtex is designed to deliver networking intelligence at every level:

Individuals

Remember the context behind every connection and nurture the relationships that matter most — effortlessly.

Teams

Share a collective memory of who knows whom, surface warm introductions, and never let an opportunity fall through the cracks.

Organizations

Understand the full reach of your network, uncover hidden connections, and turn relationship data into a strategic advantage.

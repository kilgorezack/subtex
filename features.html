Admin & Enterprise Features

Everything you need
to run events at scale.

Event Creation & Management
Create events directly from the admin portal. Set attendee limits, configure event details, and manage everything from one central dashboard — from a team lunch to a multi-day conference.
Attendee Invitations
Invite members and guests directly from the portal. Subtex handles onboarding — attendees get set up and arrive ready to connect. No manual coordination required.
Admin Meeting Briefs
Give attendees context before the event starts. Admins create and distribute meeting briefs so your team walks in knowing who to meet and what to talk about — not figuring it out on the fly.
Team Management
Invite team members, assign admin roles, and manage seats from the portal. Add or remove members anytime. Admins have full visibility into who's active and how seats are being used.
Network Insights
See how your organization's network grows across every event. Who connected with whom, which events drove the most engagement, and where the strongest relationship clusters are forming.
Org-Level Usage Reporting
Track participation, connection rates, and engagement trends over time. Share reports with leadership showing the real impact of your events — not just attendance, but actual connections made.

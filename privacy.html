Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

Subtex ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Subtex mobile application and any related services (collectively, the "Service").

Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of the Service.

The short version: Subtex is a networking app. We collect only what we need to make connections work, we don't sell your data, and you're always in control of what you share.

01

Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Profile information you choose to share, such as your name, job title, company, email address, phone number, and social links
  • Notes and tags you add to contacts after connecting
  • Any feedback or correspondence you send to us

Information collected automatically:

  • Device information such as device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • Usage data including features accessed, connection events, and in-app activity (never the content of your notes)
  • Crash reports and diagnostic data to help us improve the app

Information from QR code exchanges:

When you scan another user's QR code, or they scan yours, we facilitate the exchange of the profile information each party has chosen to share. You control exactly what appears on your profile and what is exchanged.

02

How We Use Your Information

2.1 — Processing Purposes

We process collected information for the following purposes:

  • Account creation, authentication, and maintenance
  • Provision of Application features and functionality
  • Delivery of personalized content and notifications
  • Application performance optimization and error resolution
  • Usage pattern analysis and service improvement
  • Security monitoring and fraud prevention

We do not use your data to serve third-party advertisements, and we do not sell your personal information to any third party.

2.2 — Access to User Content

Authorized Subtex personnel may access User Content and associated account information when reasonably necessary for:

  1. a
    Service Provision: Enabling social features, displaying User Content to connected contacts, and facilitating user interactions.
  2. b
    Technical Support: Providing customer assistance, troubleshooting technical issues, and responding to user inquiries.
  3. c
    Content Moderation: Enforcing Terms of Service, preventing abuse, detecting policy violations, and maintaining community safety.
  4. d
    Legal Compliance: Complying with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, or governmental requests.
  5. e
    Service Enhancement: Analyzing usage patterns, developing features, and improving Application functionality.
  6. f
    Security Operations: Protecting Application integrity and preventing fraudulent, abusive, or illegal activity.

All personnel with access to User Content are subject to strict confidentiality obligations. Users acknowledge that the Company cannot guarantee absolute privacy or security of transmitted or stored information.

2.3 — Content Moderation Rights

Subtex reserves the right, without obligation, to:

  • Monitor, screen, review, edit, or remove any User Content at any time without prior notice
  • Investigate potential violations of our Terms of Service or this Policy
  • Impose sanctions against Users who violate our policies, including content removal, account suspension, or termination
  • Disclose User Content to law enforcement or third parties when required by law or necessary to protect our rights, property, or safety

Users may report policy-violating content through designated reporting mechanisms. Subtex reviews reported content at its sole discretion and makes no representations regarding the detection or removal of objectionable material.

03

How We Share Your Information

We only share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With other users you connect with

    When a QR exchange occurs, the profile fields you've enabled are shared with the other party — and vice versa. You control what's on your profile.

  • Service providers

    We work with trusted third-party vendors (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics, AI processing) who are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only to provide services on our behalf.

  • Legal requirements

    We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Subtex, our users, or others.

  • Business transfers

    If Subtex is acquired or merges with another company, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before your data becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

04

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the Service. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or legitimate business purposes.

Notes and tags you create are stored on your device and in your account. You may delete them at any time within the app.

Third-Party Data Processors

We work with the following third-party processors who may store or process your data on our behalf:

  • Google LLC

    Including cloud infrastructure, AI, and related services for data hosting, storage, analytics, and application functionality.

  • Microsoft Corporation

    For analytics and performance monitoring to improve the user experience.

Each processor is bound by data processing agreements and is required to handle your data in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

05

Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These include encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest, access controls, and regular security reviews.

No method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security. If you become aware of any security vulnerabilities, please contact us at feedback@getsubtex.com.

06

Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions
  • Portability: Request a machine-readable copy of your data
  • Opt-out: Opt out of any optional data collection or communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at feedback@getsubtex.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the Subtex app under Settings → Account → Delete Account.

07

Children's Privacy

Subtex is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or 16 in certain jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately at feedback@getsubtex.com and we will delete it promptly.

Your continued use of the Service after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
